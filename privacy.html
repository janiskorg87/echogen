Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect information when you download our iOS/Android applications from App Store/Google Play, purchase ESP32 hardware with our MIDI firmware, and use our website. This includes app usage data, purchase information, and website analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to provide our MIDI applications, process hardware orders, improve our software, send order confirmations, and comply with App Store/Google Play requirements.

3. Third-Party Services

Our iOS apps are distributed through Apple App Store, Android apps through Google Play Store. ESP32 hardware is manufactured by Waveshare - we only provide MIDI firmware. Each service has its own privacy policy.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, Waveshare is responsible for hardware-related data security as the manufacturer.

5. Your Rights

You have rights to access, update, or delete your personal information. For app-related data, contact us. For hardware warranty data, contact Waveshare directly.

6. Contact Us

For privacy questions regarding our MIDI software and apps, contact us at privacy@echopais.com. For hardware privacy concerns, contact Waveshare directly.
