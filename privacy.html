Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our MIDI applications and hardware services. This includes app usage data when you download our iOS/Android applications from official app stores, purchase information when you buy our ESP32 hardware with custom firmware, and basic website analytics to improve our services.

Important: Our MIDI applications do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, audio recordings, or musical compositions. All MIDI processing happens locally on your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely to: provide our MIDI applications and firmware updates, process hardware orders and shipping, provide customer support for our products, improve our software based on usage patterns, and comply with App Store and Google Play requirements.

3. Third-Party Services & Hardware

Our iOS apps are distributed through Apple App Store, and Android apps through Google Play Store. Each platform has its own privacy policies that govern app downloads and purchases.

Hardware Disclaimer: ECHOPAIS purchases ESP32 hardware from Waveshare and other manufacturers through regular commercial channels (including Amazon). We are not authorized resellers or partners of these manufacturers. We modify the hardware by installing our custom MIDI firmware and resell the complete units.

Original hardware warranties and support remain with the manufacturers (Waveshare, etc.). ECHOPAIS provides warranty and support only for our custom MIDI firmware and software modifications.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect any information we collect. However, since we purchase hardware through regular retail channels, we have no control over or responsibility for any data handling by the original hardware manufacturers.

5. Your Rights

You have rights to access, update, or delete any personal information we may have collected. For app-related data, contact us directly. For any hardware-related data concerns, you would need to contact the original manufacturers (Waveshare, etc.) as we are not their authorized representatives.

6. Contact Us

For privacy questions regarding our MIDI software, applications, or firmware, contact us at: privacy@echopais.com

For hardware-related privacy concerns, contact the original manufacturers directly as we are independent resellers who modify and resell their products.
