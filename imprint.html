Business Activities

Echopais specializes in the development of professional MIDI applications for iOS and Android platforms, as well as the distribution of custom hardware solutions for music production.

Software Development: We create and maintain professional MIDI applications including scalers, editors, and control interfaces for mobile devices.

Hardware Distribution: We source hardware components from various certified manufacturers globally and integrate them with our custom MIDI firmware to create complete solutions for musicians and producers.

Product Responsibilities

MIDI Software & Applications: All mobile applications, firmware, and software solutions are developed, maintained, and supported exclusively by Echopais.

Hardware Components: Physical hardware components are sourced from various certified manufacturers worldwide. Original hardware warranties and manufacturing support are provided by the respective manufacturers.

Integrated Solutions: Echopais provides warranty and support for the integration of custom firmware with hardware components, creating complete MIDI control solutions.
